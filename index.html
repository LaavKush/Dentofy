TOP TIPS FROM DOCTORS

At Accent Dental teeth whitening is offered in-office by appointment. Your Accent dentist will apply the hydrogen peroxide whitening gel to the surface of the teeth and then shine either a whitening lamp or laser on the area, enhancing the effectiveness of the gel and strongly improving results.

Teeth whitening in Prince George is very popular, so be sure to book your appointment well in advance.

We offer in office as well as take home whitening kits.

READ MORE

Implants are used extensively for people who have no teeth at all. In fact, this can often be where the advantages of dental implants "shine" the most. Gone are the days of accepting ill-fitting dentures and a less than ideal smile.

Dental implants can be used to secure loose dentures with a connection that can be removed by the patient. There are also treatment options available that are fixed, just like real teeth, to the mouth and can only be removed by your dentist for cleaning and maintenance.

Accent Dental offers flexible financial options to make dental care affordable for every patient. We work with most major insurance providers and also offer direct billing for your convenience.

Ask us about our payment plans and financing options to spread the cost of your dental treatment over manageable monthly payments with no hidden fees.

Clear braces (Invisalign) are a virtually invisible way to straighten teeth without the discomfort and inconvenience of traditional metal braces. Custom-made clear aligners gradually move your teeth into their ideal position.

Because they are removable, you can eat, drink, brush and floss normally during treatment. Most patients complete treatment in 12–18 months.

READ MORE

Laser dentistry is a precise and effective way to perform many dental procedures. It can dramatically reduce discomfort both during and after the procedure, and is minimally invasive compared to traditional methods.

Applications include cavity treatment, gum reshaping, teeth whitening acceleration, and treatment of mouth sores — all with faster healing times.

READ MORE

Porcelain veneers are wafer-thin shells bonded to the front surface of teeth to improve their colour, shape, size, or length. They are custom-made to match your natural teeth and can transform your smile in as little as two visits.

Veneers are a popular choice for correcting stains, chips, gaps, and slightly misaligned teeth — delivering a beautiful, long-lasting smile.
