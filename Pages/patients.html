Patient Education

Patient education is the process by which health professionals and others impart information to patients and their caregivers that helps improve their health, oral hygiene habits, and overall well-being.

One of the most important purposes of patient education is to empower patients to make informed decisions about their dental care. Understanding preventive practices, treatment options, and proper oral hygiene techniques can help you maintain a healthy smile for life.

Financial Information

The cost of your treatment may vary depending on your individual needs and treatment plan. During your visit, we'll discuss the cost of your treatment, available payment options, and help you choose the option that best fits your needs and budget.

Insurance

Understanding dental insurance can sometimes be confusing, but our team is here to help you make the most of your benefits.

While we cannot determine your exact insurance coverage, we are happy to assist by explaining your treatment plan, helping you understand your benefits, and providing the information you need to discuss coverage with your insurance provider.

Dental benefit plans are agreements between your employer and insurance company. Coverage varies from plan to plan, and patients are ultimately responsible for understanding their own benefits and limitations.

If you have any questions, please contact us through our online form or call us at (250) 563-4939. Our team will be happy to assist you.
